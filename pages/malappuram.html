Malappuram

Kolayi View Point

Kolayi View Point

Kolayi is a beautiful destination in Malappuram district of Kerala. A view point and a water fall are the main attractions of this place. There is a large grass land with trees and hill view. This is a perfect place to spend an evening with your friends. The view is so good as you can take a lot of good pics here with different backgrounds. Its a calm and quiet place to get relaxed. The viewpoint looks amazing in the night as well. There will be fog and mist if you visit early in the morning.

Kottakkunnu

Kottakkunnu

Kottakkunnu is a tourist destination in the state of Kerala located at Malappuram. Stationed right at the heart of the city of Malappuram, Kottakkunnu attracts many domestic as well as international tourists.

Chamakkayam Riverside Park

Chamakkayam Riverside Park

Kottakkunnu, Known as Marine Drive of Malappuram is a hill garden. Kottakkunnu park contains an Open Air theater, Lalitha Kala Academy Art gallery, a water theme park, Adventure Park, Kids traffic park, Balloon Park, 16D Cinema. There is a Water fountain and Laser show every weekend and on festivals like Eid, Onam, New year eve, etc. At Kottakkunnu one can see traces of the first fort built by the Zamorins. This tourism destination turned historic hill attracts lakhs of people per annum.
